Questions everyone asks

❓ But isn't artificial intelligence cold and soulless?
The soul is yours — and God's. The AI is only the instrument that delivers care faster, anytime. It's like the phone that connects you to those you love: no one thinks the phone is cold. What matters is the intention, the care, and the biblical foundation behind every answer.
❓ Does My Soul Care replace therapy or my church?
No, and it doesn't want to. It walks alongside you. It's care for the "in-between" — for the moment when you can't reach anyone, but you also don't want to be alone. Therapy, church and My Soul Care can (and should) coexist.
❓ Does it really work anytime?
Yes. No appointments, no waiting line, no holidays. Late at night, Sunday morning, Monday at 7am — care is available whenever your soul asks for it.
❓ What if I don't know which plan to choose?
Start with Essential. It already delivers the essentials (the name doesn't lie). As care becomes a habit, you move up to Complete or Advanced — and on the annual plan, the daily cost is less than a cup of coffee.
❓ Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, you can. No fine print and no drama. The idea here is that you stay because you want to — because you feel the care making a difference — not because you're trapped.
